How it works

A small, repeating weekly loop. Built to compound.

No bootcamp drama. No certificate trophy. Just one loop, every week, that turns watching into doing into results you can point at.

1

Watch a short, practical video

Five to twelve minutes, recorded, designed to be useful before it is over.

2

Download the prompt, template or guide

A real artefact you can paste, edit and run today — not abstract advice.

3

Apply it to a real work or business task

You finish the lesson with one output: a real document, plan or message you can ship.

4

Attend a live session for clarity

Weekly facilitator-led troubleshooting and feedback. Bring the work, leave with the fix.

5

Post a win, question or challenge

Community is the accountability layer. Other members see your work. You see theirs.

6

Complete the weekly challenge

One small structured exercise. Tracked. Visible. Building toward your 90-day transformation.

The 90-day journey

From confusion to confidence in 90 days. No theatrics.

Members run on this rhythm. It is not a course you finish; it is a habit you build.

01
Day 1–7

Quick Win

Set up your tools. Complete one real task better or faster than before. The first proof that AI works for your work.

02
Day 8–30

Confidence

Complete beginner modules, attend a live session, post in community. You stop asking "can AI do this?" and start saying "AI is doing this."

03
Day 31–60

Habit

Use AI weekly or daily. Build one repeatable workflow for your role or business. The micro-loop becomes muscle memory.

04
Day 61–90

Transformation

Complete one small productivity or business project. Document the value. You are no longer learning AI — you are deploying it.
