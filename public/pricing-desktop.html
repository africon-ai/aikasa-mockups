FAQ

Answers, calmly.

How long is the founding offer open?

The Founding Member window runs through June 2026. After it closes, AIKASA opens to public subscriptions at standard pricing — with launch promo discounts for the first few weeks.

Founding pricing is only available before official launch in August/September 2026.

What happens if my payment fails?

If a renewal payment fails, you keep access for a 3 to 7 day grace period while we send automatic reminders. As soon as the payment goes through, access is restored automatically — nothing to chase, nothing to email.

If the grace period passes without payment, the account moves to read-only until you renew.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Monthly plans cancel at the end of the current billing month. Annual plans run for the year you paid for. Founding Member access runs for the prepaid 3-month or annual term, then renews at standard pricing minus your loyalty discount.

What's the difference between Tier 1 and Tier 2?

Tier 1: AI-Ready is core learning — recorded videos, weekly live group sessions, weekly challenges, the AIKASA Toolbox, community and AI Updates. The full learning surface.

Tier 2: AI Transformation adds the weekly implementation clinic, tier-specific deeper sessions, priority support, advanced Toolbox content and a direct feedback channel into the product team. Built for members ready to deploy AI to real outputs.

What if I pay in GHS but live abroad?

Founding pricing is shown in both GHS and USD. Pay in whichever currency suits you — Paystack handles cards and mobile money. Your access does not depend on which currency you used.

Is there a refund policy?

Founding payments fund the build. They are non-refundable once the founding window closes, but you keep your founding access for the full prepaid term — with the loyalty discount on renewal afterwards.

Standard subscriptions are pay-as-you-go — cancel anytime and you simply stop being billed at the end of the cycle.
