FAQ

Answers, calmly.

How long is the founding offer open?

The Founding Member window runs through June 2026. After it closes, AIKASA opens to public subscriptions at standard pricing — with launch promo discounts for the first few weeks.

What happens if my payment fails?

You keep access for a 3 to 7 day grace period while we send automatic reminders. As soon as the payment goes through, access is restored automatically.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Monthly plans cancel at the end of the billing month. Annual plans run for the year you paid for. Founding access runs for the prepaid term, then renews at standard pricing minus your loyalty discount.

Tier 1 vs Tier 2 — what's different?

Tier 1: AI-Ready is core learning, weekly group sessions, community and Toolbox.

Tier 2: AI Transformation adds the implementation clinic, deeper sessions, priority support, advanced Toolbox and a direct feedback channel.

GHS or USD — does it matter?

No. Founding pricing is shown in both. Pay in whichever suits you — Paystack handles cards and mobile money.
