How to refine a Gamma deck

9 min with Kojo Asante In progress
AIKASA AI that works
03:08 / 09:00
Section 1

What you'll do

  • Take a raw, AI-generated Gamma deck and apply five targeted refinement prompts that tighten the structure without losing your voice.
  • Cut every slide's word count by roughly 60% while keeping the story intact — the test is whether a stranger can still follow.
  • Save your five favourite prompts as a personal playbook you'll reuse for every Gamma deck after this one.
Section 2

The 5 prompts you'll use

  1. Refine slide 3 — make it 70% less wordy. Keep the structure. Audience is a Ghanaian SME owner who skims, not reads.

  2. Rewrite the headline on each slide as a single, punchy sentence under 9 words. No buzzwords. No "leverage" or "synergy". Plain language.

  3. For slides 5 to 8, replace every bullet list longer than 3 items with one strong sentence + one number. Keep the number specific.

  4. Audit the deck for tone consistency. Flag any slide that sounds like a different person wrote it, and tell me which slide is the odd one out and why.

  5. Suggest one "objection slide" I'm missing — the thing a sceptical investor would ask after slide 4 — and draft both the question and a 40-word answer.

Section 3

After watching

Open your own messiest Gamma deck and run prompt 1 against slide 3. Save the result. We'll compare in Thursday's clinic.
